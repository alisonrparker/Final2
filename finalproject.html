Solar Smart SF

Welcome to Solar Smart SF!

We strive to create a greener city by helping you get an idea of what green will look like via solar panels.

When you enter your neighborhood, income, and family size, we calculate your estimated cost of installation, and whether solar panels will be a good match for your household.

Using factors such as solar generating potential, long-term electricity costs, and local and federal subsidies, we give you an estimate of how much it may cost you to go solar.
